How it works
  • Automatic Generation: On the selected day of each month, the system creates fee records for all active students based on their assigned fee structure.
  • Due Date: Fee becomes due after the configured offset days (e.g., generation on 1st with 15 days offset → due on 16th).
  • Late Fee: After the due date, this fixed amount is added per day to overdue unpaid dues automatically.
  • Manual Generation: Click the button above to force generation for current month (useful for testing or after setup).
